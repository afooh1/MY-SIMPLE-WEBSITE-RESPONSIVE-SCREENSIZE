About Me

Hello! My name is Babatunde Olatunde, and I’m a web development enthusiast currently diving deep into Full Stack Web Development at Conestoga College in Ontario, Canada. I am from Lagos, Nigeria and I have a background in Computer Science from the University of Ilorin, Nigeria. I’m passionate about building modern, user-friendly websites.

I love working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery, and I’m always eager to learn new frameworks and technologies. Right now, I’m focused on creating engaging, accessible, and responsive web applications.

Beyond coding, I enjoy problem-solving, collaborating on projects, and exploring creative UI/UX design. I’m currently working on projects like an Art Gallery website and an online game shop called "Game One."

When I’m not coding, I enjoy discovering new music, watching football, plane spotting, flying drones, and spending time with loved ones.

My favorite quote is one I learned from my brother-in-law: "Life is a journey, and we will all get to our destination with time." With this in mind, I believe I can bring ideas to life, one line of code at a time.
